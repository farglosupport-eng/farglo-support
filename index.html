Standard and Studio

Start with the full editor. Add Studio for production work.

Standard

For finishing one grade carefully.

  • Layered editing for up to 10 active photos
  • Primary controls, curves, HSL, Color Mixer, Density, Memory Color, and Film Response
  • Atelier Mix, grain, halation, vignette, crop, rotate, frame, and local masks
  • Before/After comparison, JPEG export, and up to 5 saved presets

FARGLO Studio

For repeat editing, LUTs, and delivery.

  • Up to 100 active photos and unlimited saved presets
  • Import .cube LUTs as adjustable nodes and export 33-point .cube LUTs from color nodes
  • Studio Layers, selective copy/paste, and Studio Apply across photos
  • HEIC, 16-bit TIFF, social layouts, before/after contact sheets, and Pro Color Negative Lab

Layer workflow

A color grade should stay editable.

Most mobile editors bake your look into the pixels the moment you tap. FARGLO keeps every decision live. Import once, build a stack of adjustments, and reopen any layer days later — nothing is flattened, nothing is lost.

  1. 01Import RAW or JPEGFrom a Hasselblad RAW to an iPhone JPEG — the same non-destructive pipeline.
  2. 02Build the grade in layersPrimaries, curves, Atelier Mix, halation and grain — stacked and reorderable.
  3. 03Re-edit anytimeToggle, retune or remove any layer. The original is always intact underneath.
